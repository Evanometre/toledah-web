Why small businesses fail silently

You know the business.
But the system is failing you.

You're losing money every day without a proper system, and you're building no record of what you've actually done.

PAIN — 01
The supplier said "no."
The bank said "no."
Because your business had no record.
Three years in business. Real customers. Real turnover. Real reputation in your market. But when you approach a supplier for better terms, or a lender for working capital, the answer is still no — because nothing is written down in a way anyone outside your head can verify. Your track record is invisible to everyone who matters.
"I've been trading for years. I just can't prove it to anyone."
How Zaena fixes this
ZAENA Business Performance Report A real-time financial profile auto-generated from your live transaction data. Verifiable. Non-editable. Shareable with suppliers and lenders.
Trust Score Built from real transaction history — your loan ceiling, recovery rate, and inventory accuracy, calculated automatically and updated in real time.
PAIN — 02
You don't actually know how much you made last month.
Sales happened. Money came in. Some went out for stock, some for expenses, some you took out yourself. But right now, if someone asked "what was your profit last month?" — you'd be guessing. The notebook doesn't add up. The spreadsheet stopped getting updated two weeks ago. The number in your head doesn't account for stock shrinkage.
"I knew I was busy. I didn't know I was barely profitable."
How Zaena fixes this
Live profit dashboard Every sale recorded. Every expense tracked. Real profit, not guessed profit — on your phone screen, always.
Profit allocation Automatically separates what's yours to take home from what belongs in the business. Stop guessing what you can spend.
PAIN — 03
Your staff know things you don't — and that's a problem.
You trust your team. But trust without visibility is just hope. When you're not at the shop, you have no way of knowing what's happening. Stock that "went missing." Discounts given that weren't authorised. A sale completed but the money didn't come in the right amount. You find out weeks later, when the numbers no longer match. By then, it's too late.
"I only found out something was wrong when the stock didn't match."
How Zaena fixes this
Role-based access control Cashiers see what cashiers need. Managers see more. Only you see everything. Set it once, monitor from anywhere.
Complete audit trail Every transaction, edit, and deletion — timestamped. Nothing disappears quietly. Review what happened while you were out.
