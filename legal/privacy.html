Privacy

Toledah and its affiliates offer a suite of business management tools (ERP) and advanced data analytics services designed to help you scale your business. As part of operating these services, we collect data about you and your business. This data is essential for running our services, providing meaningful business insights, and ensuring the security of our platform. This policy explains what information is collected, why it is collected, and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

Most of the personal data we collect is directly provided by our users when they register and use the Toledah ERP or Analytics platform. Account & Contact Data: When you register for Toledah ERP, start a free trial, or request a demo, you provide us with information such as your name, company name, email address, phone number, and business sector. We never store credit card information; we use PCI-DSS-compliant third-party processors. Customer Databases (ERP Data): When you use the Toledah ERP, any information you upload (employee lists, inventory, financial records, customer contacts) is your own. You retain full ownership and control. We only process this data on your behalf. Analytics Data: For our Analytics product, we collect and process data derived from your ERP usage or external data sources you connect. This includes transaction volumes, performance metrics, and user interaction patterns. Browser & Geolocation Data: We record your IP address, browser type, and geolocation to customize your experience and for security purposes. Interaction Recording: We may record session interactions (clicks, navigation) during trial periods to improve our User Interface (UI), provided you have opted in.
